Top 5 Places to Visit in Kerala

1. Munnar

Famous for tea plantations, misty hills, and cool climate.

Famous Food: Cardamom tea, Kerala Sadya

Best Hotel: Tea County by KTDC

2. Alleppey

Known as the Venice of the East, enjoy houseboat cruises in the backwaters.

Famous Food: Karimeen Pollichathu, Puttu & Kada la Curry

Best Hotel: Punnamada Resort

3. Wayanad

Explore wildlife, waterfalls, and spice plantations.

Famous Food: Malabar Birya, Bamboo rice payasam

Best Hotel: Vythiri Resort

4. Kochi

A blend of modern city life and colonial charm. Explore Fort Kochi, art cafes and beaches.

Famous Food: Fish curry, Appam with stew

Best Hotel: Brunton Boatyard

5. Varkala

Famous for its cliffs, beaches, and natural springs.

Famous Food: Seafood, Kerala Parotta

Best Hotel: The Gateway Hotel
